◆ startSample

ULWord FRAME_STAMP_STRUCT::startSample

The actual start sample when this frame was started in VBI This may be used to check sync against audioInStartAddress (Play) or audioOutStartAddress (Record). In record it will always be equal, but in playback if the clocks drift or the user supplies non aligned audio sizes, then this will give the current difference from expected vs actual position. To be useful, play audio must be clocked in at the correct rate.
